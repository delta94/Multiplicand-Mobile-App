A polynomial is an expression that can be built from constants and symbols called indeterminates or variables by means of addition, multiplication and exponentiation to a non-negative integer power. Two such expressions that may be transformed, one to the other, by applying the usual properties of commutativity, associativity and distributivity of addition and multiplication are considered as defining the same polynomial.

Types Of Algebraic Equation

Types Meaning
Monomial Expression which is having only one term is known as monomial
Eg: \(5x, 3a, 12k ...\) etc
Binomial Expression which is having two terms is known as binomial.
Eg: \(3x-2,4x^{2} - 5\)
Trinomial Expression which is having three terms is known as trinomial.
Eg: \(4x^{2}+ 7 x - 6 , 5y^{3}+ 2 y - 6\)

Polynomial evaluation can be used to compute the remainder of polynomial division by a polynomial of degree one, because the remainder of the division of \(f(x)\) by \((x - a)\) is \(f(a)\).This is more efficient than the usual algorithm of division when the quotient is not needed.

  • A sum of polynomials is a polynomial
  • A product of polynomials is a polynomial
  • A composition of two polynomials is a polynomial, which is obtained by substituting a variable of the first polynomial by the second polynomial
  • The derivative of the polynomial \(a_nx^{n}+a_{n-1}x^{n-1}+....+a_2x^{2}+a_1x^{1}+a_0\) is the polynomial \(na_nx^{n-1}+(n-1)a_{n-1}x^{n-2}+....+2a_2x+a1\). If the set of the coefficients does not contain the integers (for example if the coefficients are integers modulo some prime number p), then \(ka_k\) should be interpreted as the sum of \(a_k\) with itself, k times.
  • A primitive integral or antiderivative of the polynomial \(a_nx^{n}+a_{n-1}x^{n-1}+....+a_2x^{2}+a_1x^{1}+a_0\) is the polynomial \(\frac{a_nx^{n+1}}{n+1}+\frac{a_{n-1}x^{n}}{n}+....+\frac{a_2x^{3}}{3}+\frac{a_1x^{2}}{2}+a_0x+c\) where \(c\) is an arbitrary constant.

Polynomial functions

A polynomial function is a function that can be defined by evaluating a polynomial. More precisely, a function f of one argument from a given domain is a polynomial function if there exists a polynomial
\(a_nx^{n}+a_{n-1}x^{n-1}+....+a_2x^{2}+a_1x^{1}+a_0\)
that evaluates to \(f(x)\) for all \(x\) in the domain of \(f\).If the domain of this function is also restricted to the reals, the resulting function maps reals to reals.
Every polynomial function is continuous, smooth, and entire.
